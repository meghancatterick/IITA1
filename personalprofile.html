My Personal Profile

The Results

In assessment of my personal profile, I undertook the Myer-Briggs test, Learning Style Quiz and Creativity test.

My results were as follows:

Myer-Briggs Test:

  • Personality type: Logistician (ISTJ-A)
  • Traits: Introverted: 58%, Observant: 77%, Thinking: 61%, Judging:81%, Assertive: 53%
  • Role: Sentinel
  • Strategy: Confident Individualism

Learning Style Quiz:

Kinesthetic-INTJ learner - INTROVERTED INTUITIVE THINKING JUDGING

Creativity Test:

Creativity score of 54.87

What does this mean to me, & how will this influence my behvaiour / team work?

I believe the test results show a good reflection of what I percieve to be my personality. For me the Myers-Briggs test shows that I am a reserved / introverted person who will sit back and take an assessment of a situation before acting. However, although not overbearing in a group situation, the results show that when it comes to work I am dedicated and will drive to achieve accomplishments. Similarly, the Learning Style Quiz results show that I would learn best when there is a rigorous agenda that works towards a defined goal. I believe the results show that being most effective when working to achieve a set goal and I have the drive to go forward and accomplish this.

The Myers-Briggs test refers to 'For many Logisticians, a lack of structure offers not freedom, but chaos.'Personally, I can resonate with this statement as in my day-to-day work life, I am always looking for ways to structure-routine tasks and help manage the effectiveness of my team. For example, I currently work in recruitment where we work a high number of contract roles which have deadlines to respond by, I use tools such as our internal CRM (Bullhorn) and MS Outlook Calendar to organise reminders for these closing dates, ensuring I can manage priorities and subsequently share this information with our team to avoid missing deadlines and potentially losing roles resulting in loss of income for the business.

In terms of working in a team, the Creativity test shows moderate 'boldness' meaning the confidence to push boundaries. I would say I do not 100% agree with this evaluation as it is usually not until I know someone, will I then feel comfortable to voice concerns / 'push boundaries'. Whereas the Myers-Briggs test explains that although Logisticians do not often express emotion, we can feel the frustration and resentment when overcompensating for others in a team. With this in mind, when progressing to group work in assignment two, I will make more of a conscious effort to speak up and voice my concerns early in the project to my peers, rather than allowing for the usual building of silent frustration.
